**What the reader learns:**
- Context decay is happening to them RIGHT NOW — in their business, relationships, self-understanding
- It's not laziness or failure. It's structural. All systems decay without maintenance
- The five catastrophes are the SPECIFIC mechanisms. Once you see them, you can monitor for them
- This IS the wasteland. Not a dramatic landscape — a quiet, progressive degradation of coherence
