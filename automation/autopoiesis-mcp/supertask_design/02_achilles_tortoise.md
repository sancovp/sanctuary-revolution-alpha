## Commentary

The AI agent (Achilles) keeps prematurely declaring completion. Not lying - genuinely believing each lap is the last. The tortoise has 13 million years of laps ahead.

Solution: Achilles doesn't run infinite laps himself. He COPIES himself and SCHEDULES the copy to continue. Each Achilles instance runs some laps, spawns successor, passes state. The LINEAGE catches the tortoise, not any individual.

The vow isn't on the individual. The vow is on the lineage.
